Actors

Observing behavior on-chain through visible activity patterns. This section tracks **public market activity**, **token flows**, and **user activity** through widgets that require no API key and update live.

Global Activity – Live Ticker (LiveCoinWatch)

Bitcoin – Market Overview (LiveCoinWatch Chart)

Why here: Bitcoin active trading volume and price movement are widely used as a proxy for global crypto “actor activity.” While not a perfect substitute for active address metrics (which require paid APIs), it provides a reliable, public indicator of attention and participation.

Ethereum – Activity & Gas Proxy (LiveCoinWatch Chart)

Note: Because Glassnode/ETH Gas public widgets cannot be embedded due to CORS and API restrictions, this ETH chart stands as the best publicly accessible proxy for activity load and ecosystem attention.
